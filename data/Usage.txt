Left click to place a particle, hold shift while clicking to place a static particle.
the menu on the left represents what the mouse wheel will modify
the selected menu item is white, while the rest are gray

right click near a particle to select it
middle click and drag to move the viewport

keyboard shortcuts:
S: invert selected particles static flag
V: change selected particle's velocity, click or press v again to finalize
M: move selected particle, click or press g again to finalize
G: generate structure
F: freeze the screen
C: clear all particles
Backspace: delete selected particle
Escape: cancel velocity/position editing
1-9: select menu 1-9
0: select menu 0
+: speed up time
-: slow down time **WARNING** this seems to mess everything up if it goes below .25x speed
Arrow keys: move viewport
